Our History

From a spontaneous student revival to a nationwide Kingdom movement — witnessing God's unceasing faithfulness.

1965

The Revival Sparks

A spontaneous outpouring of the Holy Spirit swept through Ethiopian universities. Students experienced divine healing, fervent prayer, and uncontainable joy that rapidly spread into rural provinces and countryside villages.

1974

National Unity Formed

Apostolic leaders from across the nation gathered to establish a unified apostolic body. This pivotal gathering anchored solid biblical doctrine, protected congregations from heresy, and birthed the national church family.

1991

Full Constitutional Liberty

Following historical shifts in Ethiopia, the Full Gospel Believers' Church received full official government recognition on November 10, 1991. The central headquarters was established in Addis Ababa to serve millions.

Today

A Global Family

Today, our family encompasses over 4,000 local assemblies and 10 million believers across 42 regions. Yerer Full Gospel stands proudly at the forefront of this continuing apostolic revival.
