GOD'S FAITHFULNESS

A Beautiful Legacy

A divine, chronological view of how God moved from spontaneous youth prayer groups to a nationwide movement.

1965

The Fire Ignites

A spontaneous Holy Spirit revival broke out among college and university students across Ethiopia. The divine flame spread rapidly, jumping from towns to provincial countryside regions.

1974

Unifying Denomination

Church pastors and regional evangelism leaders assembled from all directions to structure a protected, unified denomination, safeguarding foundational Biblical truths.

1991

Legal Standing

With the implementation of the new national constitution, the Ethiopian Full Gospel Believers' Church received absolute, clean corporate and religious legal standing on November 10, 1991.

Present

A Radiant Family

Now boasting thousands of local sanctuaries, expansive mission fields, and multiple millions of active believers globally, Yerer Full Gospel remains at the forefront of this move of God.

Our Mission

To cultivate highly mature disciples of Jesus Christ who are empowered by the Holy Spirit. We seek to foster deep transformational changes within society through authentic worship, radical evangelism, and continuous loving outreach.

Our Vision

To see local communities and global nations thoroughly illuminated with the knowledge of God. We envision Yerer Full Gospel as a vibrant spiritual lighthouse, actively training leaders and planting healthy churches around the globe.

WHAT WE TEACH

The Doctrinal Sanctuary

Absolute Authority of Scripture

We believe that the entire Bible is the fully inspired, infallible Word of God, and serves as our absolute manual for truth, faith, and daily conduct.
2 Timothy 3:16

Salvation Through Christ Alone

Divine Baptism of the Holy Spirit
