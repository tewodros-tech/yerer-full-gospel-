About Our Church

Knowing God. Making Him Known. Since 2015.

Our Story

Yerer Full Gospel Believers Church started in 2015 with just 12 believers gathering in a small living room. Led by the Holy Spirit, we had a vision to see the full gospel of Jesus Christ preached with power and demonstration of the Spirit.

What began as a small prayer group has grown into a vibrant community of believers committed to worship, discipleship, and outreach. Today, we meet in our permanent location in Yerer, reaching hundreds of families with the love of Christ.

Our name reflects our commitment: "Full Gospel" means we believe in the whole counsel of God — salvation, Holy Spirit baptism, divine healing, and the soon return of our King.

Our Mission

To reach the unreached with the full gospel of Jesus Christ, making disciples who make disciples, and demonstrating God's love through word and deed.

Our Vision

A church where every believer operates in the power of the Holy Spirit, walking in healing, deliverance, and daily victory.

What We Believe

The core doctrines that guide our faith and practice

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired and infallible Word of God, the final authority for faith and life. (2 Timothy 3:16)

The Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. (Matthew 28:19)

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone. (Ephesians 2:8-9)

Water Baptism

We believe in water baptism by immersion as a public declaration of faith. (Matthew 28:19)

Holy Spirit Baptism

We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit with evidence of speaking in tongues. (Acts 2:4)

Divine Healing

We believe in divine healing through the atoning work of Jesus Christ. (Isaiah 53:5)

Second Coming

We believe in the soon return of Jesus Christ to rapture His church. (1 Thessalonians 4:16-17)

Holy Communion

We believe in partaking of the Lord's Supper as a remembrance of Christ's sacrifice. (1 Corinthians 11:23-26)
