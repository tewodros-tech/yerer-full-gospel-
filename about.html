Our History

From a spontaneous revival among students to a nationwide movement — God's faithfulness through the years

1965

The Revival Begins

A spontaneous gospel revival broke out among students of higher institutions across Ethiopia. The Holy Spirit moved powerfully, and the fire spread to provincial towns, small villages, and the countryside.

1974

National Organization

Leaders from across the country gathered to form a unified denomination. This brought protection from false teaching, government recognition, and a shared doctrinal foundation. The church was born.

1991

Full Recognition

With the new Ethiopian constitution, the Ethiopian Full Gospel Believers' Church received full legal recognition on November 10, 1991. The national office was established in Addis Ababa.

Today

A Global Family

Now encompassing over 4,067 local churches, 8,947 daughter churches, and more than 10 million members across 42 regions. Yerer Full Gospel is part of this mighty move of God.
