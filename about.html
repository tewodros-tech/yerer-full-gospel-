Our Story

Worshiping in Spirit & Truth

Yerer Ber Full Gospel Believers Church is a Spirit-filled community of faith located in Yerer, Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. We are a family of believers committed to worshiping God in spirit and truth, as Jesus declared in John 4:24.

Founded on the principles of the full Gospel, we believe in the power of the Holy Spirit, the authority of Scripture, and the transformative love of Jesus Christ. Our church is a place of healing, restoration, and community for all who seek a deeper relationship with God.

Doctrine

What We Believe

The foundational truths that shape our faith and practice as a Full Gospel church.

🕊️ The Holy Trinity

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

— Matthew 28:19

✝️ Salvation by Grace

Salvation is a gift of God through faith in Jesus Christ, not by works, but by grace alone.

— Ephesians 2:8-9

🔥 Baptism of the Holy Spirit

We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit, empowering believers for service and witness.

— Acts 1:8, Acts 2:4

🙌 Divine Healing

Healing for the body, soul, and mind is provided in the atonement of Christ.

— Isaiah 53:5

📖 Authority of Scripture

The Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God, the final authority for faith and practice.

— 2 Timothy 3:16-17

☁️ The Blessed Hope

We anticipate the literal second coming of Jesus Christ to reign with His saints.

— Titus 2:13
