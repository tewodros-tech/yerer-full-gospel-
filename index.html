About Us

Discover the heart and history of Yerer Full Gospel Believers Church

Our Story

Founded in 1988, Yerer Full Gospel began as a small gathering under a tree. Today, we are a thriving church of over 1,200 members from 38 nations, united in worship and mission.

Our journey has been marked by God's faithfulness — from a humble beginning to a global movement that reaches Ethiopia and beyond with the Full Gospel.

Read More →

Mission & Vision

Mission: To make disciples of all nations, baptizing them and teaching them to obey Christ's commands.

Vision: To see a generation of Spirit-filled believers transform communities and nations for God's glory.

We are committed to worship in Spirit and truth, empowered by the Holy Spirit, and driven by love.
