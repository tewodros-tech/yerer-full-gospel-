DOCTRINAL FOUNDATIONS

What We Believe

Click on each core pillar below to reveal scripture and details of our belief system.

Salvation by Grace

Through faith alone in Christ Jesus.

We believe salvation is a gift from God, entirely apart from human works, received through repentance and direct personal faith.
Ephesians 2:8-9

Holy Spirit Baptism

The empowerment of the believer.

Empowering the Church for effective witness, service, and walking in spiritual gifts.
Acts 1:8, Acts 2:4

Divine Healing

By His stripes we are made whole.

We believe healing for the physical body, soul, and mind is fully provided for in the atonement of Christ Jesus.
Isaiah 53:5

The Blessed Hope

The physical return of our Lord.

We anticipate the literal second coming of Jesus Christ to rule and reign with all His saints in glory forever.
Titus 2:13
