DOCTRINE

What We Believe

Click each pillar to reveal the scripture and depth of our belief system.

Salvation by Grace

Through faith alone in Christ Jesus

We believe salvation is a gift from God, entirely apart from human works, received through repentance and faith.
Ephesians 2:8-9

Holy Spirit Baptism

The empowerment of the believer

Empowering the Church for effective witness, service, and walking in spiritual gifts.
Acts 1:8, Acts 2:4

Divine Healing

By His stripes we are made whole

We believe healing for the body, soul, and mind is fully provided in the atonement of Christ.
Isaiah 53:5

The Blessed Hope

The physical return of our Lord

We anticipate the literal second coming of Jesus Christ to rule and reign with all His saints forever.
Titus 2:13
