FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about giving to Yerer Full Gospel Church.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, Yerer Full Gospel Church is a registered nonprofit organization. Your donations may be tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. Please consult with your tax advisor for specific guidance.

Can I give anonymously?

Absolutely. We respect your privacy. If you prefer to give anonymously, simply select the anonymous option during online giving or omit your name when giving by other methods.

How is my tithe and offering used?

Your tithe supports the church's daily operations including staff salaries, utilities, maintenance, and programs. Offerings are designated to specific causes like missions, benevolence, and building projects.

Can I set up recurring giving?

Yes! You can set up monthly, weekly, or bi-weekly recurring gifts through our online giving portal. This ensures consistent support for the ministry and simplifies your giving.

What if I can't give money? Are there other ways to serve?

Absolutely! We believe everyone has something to offer. Consider volunteering your time, skills, or resources. Contact our church office to learn about volunteer opportunities that match your gifts.
